# Email Setup Guide: hello@curatedai.net

## Option 1: Email Forwarding (Free, Recommended)

### If Your Domain is at Namecheap:

1. **Log in to Namecheap**
   - Go to [namecheap.com](https://www.namecheap.com)
   - Sign in to your account

2. **Navigate to Domain List**
   - Click **"Domain List"** in the left sidebar
   - Find `curatedai.net` and click **"Manage"**

3. **Set Up Email Forwarding**
   - Go to **"Advanced DNS"** tab
   - Scroll down to **"Email Forwarding"** section
   - Click **"Add Forwarder"**
   - **Forward From**: `hello@curatedai.net`
   - **Forward To**: Your personal email (e.g., `yourname@gmail.com`)
   - Click **"Save"**

4. **Wait for Activation**
   - Usually takes 5-30 minutes
   - Test by sending an email to `hello@curatedai.net`

### If Your Domain is at Another Registrar:

**GoDaddy:**
1. Go to **My Products** → **Domains** → `curatedai.net`
2. Click **"Email"** → **"Forwarding"**
3. Add forwarder: `hello` → your email

**Cloudflare:**
1. Go to **Email Routing** (in dashboard)
2. Enable Email Routing for `curatedai.net`
3. Add route: `hello@curatedai.net` → your email

**Google Domains:**
1. Go to **Email** → **Email forwarding**
2. Add: `hello@curatedai.net` → your email

**General Steps (Most Registrars):**
1. Find **"Email"** or **"Email Forwarding"** section
2. Add new forwarder
3. Set `hello@curatedai.net` to forward to your personal email
4. Save and wait for activation

## Option 2: Full Email Hosting (Paid)

### Google Workspace (Recommended for Professional Use)
- **Cost**: $6/month per user
- **Features**: Full Gmail with your domain, 30GB storage
- **Setup**: [workspace.google.com](https://workspace.google.com)
- **Best for**: If you want to send emails FROM `hello@curatedai.net`

### Zoho Mail (Budget Option)
- **Cost**: Free (5 users, 5GB each) or $1/month (10GB)
- **Features**: Full email hosting, webmail, mobile apps
- **Setup**: [zoho.com/mail](https://www.zoho.com/mail)
- **Best for**: Professional email on a budget

### ProtonMail (Privacy-Focused)
- **Cost**: Free (1GB) or €4.99/month (15GB)
- **Features**: Encrypted email, privacy-focused
- **Setup**: [proton.me](https://proton.me)
- **Best for**: Privacy-conscious users

1. Send an email to `hello@curatedai.net` from any email account
2. Check your forwarding email inbox

## Important Notes

- **Email Forwarding**: You can RECEIVE emails at `hello@curatedai.net`, but you'll REPLY from your personal email
- **Full Email Hosting**: You can SEND and RECEIVE from `hello@curatedai.net`
- **For Privacy Policy**: Email forwarding is sufficient (users just need to contact you)
- **For Professional Use**: Consider Google Workspace or Zoho if you want to send emails FROM the domain
