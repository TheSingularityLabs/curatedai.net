### 5. Google AdSense / Display Ads (Optional - 5-15% of total if used)
**How It Works:**
- Google AdSense or similar display ad networks
- Ads placed on pages (sidebar, between content, footer)
- Revenue per 1000 views (CPM)

**Pros:**
- Easy to set up (1-2 hours)
- Passive income (works automatically)
- Works with any traffic level
- No relationships needed

**Cons:**
- Low CPM for directories ($1-5 per 1000 views typically)
- Can hurt user experience (clutter, slower pages)
- Can hurt trust/credibility (looks less curated)
- Conflicts with "curated, not cluttered" message
- Lower revenue than affiliates/sponsorships

**Month 1-3:** $0/month (too little traffic)
**Month 4-6:** $50-200/month (if 5K-10K visitors, $1-2 CPM)
**Month 7-9:** $200-500/month (if 20K-50K visitors)
**Month 10-12:** $500-1,500/month (if 50K-150K visitors)

**Year 1 Total:** $3,000-10,000 (if used)

**Recommendation:**
- **Skip for now** - Focus on affiliates/sponsorships first (higher revenue, better UX)
- **Consider later** (Month 6+) if:
  - Traffic is high (50K+ monthly)
  - Other revenue streams are established
  - You can place ads non-intrusively
  - You're okay with the trade-off in UX/trust

**If You Do Use Ads:**
- Place in sidebar only (not between content)
- Limit to 1-2 ads per page
- Use "native" ad style (matches site design)
- Test impact on user experience
- Monitor bounce rate (ads can increase it)

### 6. Enterprise & Big Tech Partnerships (Tertiary - 10-20% of total if successful)
**How It Works:**
- Partnerships with larger AI companies (OpenAI, Google, Microsoft, etc.)
- Various partnership types beyond simple featured listings

**Partnership Types:**

#### A. Content Partnerships
- **What**: They pay you to create content about their tools/products
- **Examples**: 
  - "How to Use [Company]'s New Feature" guides
  - Case studies showing their tools
  - Tutorial videos/content
- **Pricing**: $500-5,000 per piece of content
- **Timeline**: Month 6+ (need traffic/authority first)

#### B. Product Launch Partnerships
- **What**: They pay for exclusive coverage of new product launches
- **Examples**:
  - First to review new AI model
  - Exclusive launch announcement
  - Early access reviews
- **Pricing**: $1,000-10,000 per launch
- **Timeline**: Month 9+ (need established authority)

#### C. Co-Marketing Partnerships
- **What**: Joint marketing campaigns, webinars, events
- **Examples**:
  - Co-hosted webinars
  - Joint content creation
  - Cross-promotion
- **Pricing**: $2,000-15,000 per campaign
- **Timeline**: Month 12+ (need brand recognition)

#### D. Resource Page Listings
- **What**: They pay to be listed on your "Resources" or "Partners" page
- **Examples**:
  - "Official Partners" page
  - "Recommended by [Company]" badges
  - Resource directory listings
- **Pricing**: $500-2,000/month per listing
