What is Curated AI?

Curated AI is a handpicked directory of 200+ AI tools. Every tool is personally tested and curated by The Singularity Labs. Not a submission directory, no spam, no pay-to-list.

Our Mission

Curated AI exists to help you find the right AI tool, fast. We believe in quality over quantity. That's why Curated AI focuses on handpicked tools that actually work, with detailed reviews, comparisons, and guides to help you make informed decisions.

The Singularity Labs personally tests and evaluates every tool before adding it to our directory. Every tool is handpicked, not submitted.

Our Policy

  • → No paid listings: We do not accept payment to list tools. Every tool is included based on merit and quality, not payment.
  • → No submissions: We do not accept tool submissions. Every tool is discovered, tested, and handpicked by The Singularity Labs.
  • → Only verified tools: Every tool must be personally tested and verified before being added. We test functionality, quality, and real-world performance.
  • → Future sponsors: If we introduce sponsorships in the future, sponsored content will be clearly labeled. However, all tools remain handpicked and verified - sponsorships will not influence which tools are included.

What Makes Curated AI Different?

  • → Handpicked, not submitted: Every tool in Curated AI is personally tested and selected by The Singularity Labs. See our policy below for details.
  • → Expert curation: The Singularity Labs has deep expertise in AI tools. We test each tool's capabilities, quality, and real-world performance before adding it.
  • → No spam, no clutter: Curated AI focuses on quality tools that deliver real value. You won't find low-quality or spammy listings here.
  • → Detailed insights: Each tool includes detailed reviews, use cases, strengths, limitations, and comparisons with alternatives.
  • → Expert guides: 72+ guides and tutorials written by The Singularity Labs, who actually uses these tools in production.
  • → 248+ tested prompts: Production-ready AI prompts, personally tested and organized by tool and use case.
